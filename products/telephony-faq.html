Can I make International calls?

Yes. Just call our office for rate information and we will activate your ability to make International calls. There is no charge to have this service activated on your line. Please see this page for our current international billing rates.

Do I have to pay any fees to FCC?

No, currently there is no legislation that allows the FCC to apply charges to VoIP systems.

Will I be charged any tax on my phone service?

No, VoIP systems are not taxable at this time. There are taxes on cable television and internet services.

Before Install

Can I keep my current phone number?

No, you will be assigned a new phone number.
BACK TO TOP

Can I get an Unlisted number?

Yes, just make sure to inform us when you call to setup service. This is a free service if mentioned at the time of setup.

Do I have to be home to have the telephone service installed?

Yes. When you call to setup an appointment, you will be given a two hour time frame of when the technician will arrive.
BACK TO TOP

What is done at the time of installation?

Our technician will install a modem and a telephone adapter inside your apartment, along with any other service you had requested.
BACK TO TOP

Do I need a special telephone?

No, we will install a telephone adapter in your apartment that will allow you to use any phone that is compatible with a traditional telephone service.
BACK TO TOP

Do I need a computer?

No, you do not need a computer or internet access to use our phone service. Our technician will install a modem and telephone adapter in your apartment. If you have a computer you may subscribe to our High Speed Internet access as well.
BACK TO TOP

Can I watch TV and make a phone call at the same time?

Yes, in fact if you have our internet service you can do both simultaneously.

This phone system requires power to the equipment installed in the apartment to make and receive phone calls. If you provide your own backup power supply to the equipment you will be able to use your phone during a power outage. For details, please contact our technical support department.
